About Me

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, Aalto University, supervised by Prof. Juho Kannala. Previously, I held a visiting researcher position at Computer Vision and Geometry Group, ETH zurich (2022), advised by Prof. Marc Pollefeys and Dr. Daniel Barath . Before this, I received my master's degree in Control, Robotics, and Autonomous System from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Aalto University.

My research interests lie in geometric computer vision, and 3D scene understanding, with a special interest in visual localization, image matching, and 3D visual Grounding.
