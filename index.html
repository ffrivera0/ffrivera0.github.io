About Me

I recently completed my doctoral degree in the Department of Computer Science, Aalto University, supervised by Prof. Juho Kannala. Previously, I was a research intern at NAVER LABS Europe (Grenoble, France), working with Dr. Jerome Revaud and Dr. Boris Chidlovskii. Before this, I held a visiting researcher position at Computer Vision and Geometry Group, ETH zurich (2022), advised by Prof. Marc Pollefeys and Dr. Daniel Barath. I received my master's degree in Control, Robotics, and Autonomous System from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Aalto University.

My research interests lie in 3D computer vision and deep learning, with a special interest in visual localization, image matching, 3D reconstruction, and 3D foundation model.
