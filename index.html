About Me

I'm a second-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science (opens new window), Aalto University (opens new window), supervised by Prof. Juho Kannala (opens new window). Before that, I received my master's degree in Control, Robotics, and Autonomous System from Department of Electrical Engineering and Automation (opens new window), Aalto University.

My research is mainly focus on geometric computer vision, image matching, visual localisation, 3D reconstruction, and continuous learning.
